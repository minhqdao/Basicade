# Licence and provenance status

This collection contains historical BASIC listings associated with David Ahl’s
*BASIC Computer Games* era. The original import did not retain a file-by-file
source URL, so Basicade does not claim authorship or relicense these listings.

David Ahl later stated that he had placed the publications he wrote or edited
into the public domain; see the [reported 2022 declaration](https://blog.adafruit.com/2022/06/16/david-ahl-places-all-his-classic-computing-publications-into-the-public-domain/)
and an [account of permission for these games](https://troypress.com/play-basic-computer-games-online/).
The listings are maintained and distributed here strictly for historical,
educational, and archival preservation. Preserve their attribution and record
the source edition when improving a transcription.
